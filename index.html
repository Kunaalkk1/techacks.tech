About

TecHacks, a student-run hackathon, facilitated by Chitkara University in Punjab, was founded in 2019 by the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering with 110 participants and expanded to 350 participants in 2020. TecHacks 3.0, as a hybrid Open-source hackathon, will give participants from various technical backgrounds the opportunity to engage with a wonderful community of hackers while also providing seminars and mentorship from industry leaders.

Mission

Our primary goal is to solve problems. Modern challenges necessitate modern solutions, and young hackers from colleges and universities have a lot to offer in this arena. They require a platform to encourage them to contribute their talents. We want to provide them a level playing field to compete with other hackers and come up with innovative ideas.

FAQ

Is the event online or offline?

The online phase for all participants begins on 27th November 2022. The final event is hybrid, participants can join online or offline. The hybrid mode of the hackathon will be held on 1st and 2nd December 2022.

Is it an individual event or a team event?

Participants are to register individually. If you already have a team, please ask your teammates to register too. If you don't have one, teams will be formed from 25th November 2022 on basis of participants' skills and interests and on Discord. The team size should be between 3 to 5 members.

What is the registration fee for the event?

The registration fee for the event is ₹0 per participant. We are going to be completely free for all hackers out there!

When can we start working on the solution?

The inaugural event is on November 27, and you can start working on your solution thereafter.

Will there be an elimination round?

There elimination round will not be in offline mode, however, in online mode, if we have a large number of teams, there will be a preliminary round to shortlist teams in online mode.

How do I reach Chitkara University?

The closest railway station to reach Chitkara University is Chandigarh Railway Station or Rajpura Junction. The nearest airport is the Chandigarh Airport. You can take a cab or an auto from these places. You can also find buses from Chandigarh Sector 43 Bus Stand.

What all arrangements will be made at the venue?

Before and after the event, we will help with food, accomodation or any other requirement on prior request by email to info@techacks.tech.

My query is not listed here. What do I do?

If your query is not resolved yet, you always have an option to write us an email to info@techacks.tech. Feel free to use that option!
